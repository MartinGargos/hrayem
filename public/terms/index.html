Terms of Service

These terms describe the basic rules for creating an account, joining or organizing games, chatting with other confirmed players, and using Hrayem responsibly.

Using the service

Hrayem is a matchmaking app for racket-sport players. You may use it to create events, join events, post availability, manage your profile, chat with eligible event participants, and report harmful or abusive behavior.

Your account

You are responsible for the accuracy of the information you publish in the app and for keeping your credentials private. You must complete the required profile fields before you can use the authenticated parts of the product.

Player conduct

  • Do not impersonate other players or create fake/spam events.
  • Do not use event chat for harassment, abuse, or illegal content.
  • Respect the organizer, other confirmed players, and venue rules.
  • Use reports in good faith when you believe a player or event needs manual review.

Events, waitlists, and chat

Event joins, waitlists, promotions, reminders, and chat access follow the current app rules described inside Hrayem. Waitlisted players do not get chat access. Finished-event chat stays writable for a limited post-game window, while cancelled-event chat becomes read-only immediately.

Reports and moderation

Hrayem supports event reports, player reports, and organizer no-show reports. MVP moderation is manual: reports create an internal review record and notify the admin review mailbox. Hrayem may limit, suspend, or delete abusive accounts when needed to protect the community or preserve system integrity.

Availability and service changes

Availability posts are lightweight signals, not bookings or guarantees. Hrayem may update product behavior, event rules, and launch-readiness infrastructure over time. If a change materially affects consent, the app can require renewed acceptance before continued use.

Account deletion

You can delete your account from the Settings screen in the app. Deletion is irreversible. Future organized events are cancelled, future joined events are cleaned up, availability and push registrations are removed, and historical records needed for integrity remain anonymized as “Deleted User”.

Service availability

Hrayem aims to be reliable, but the service may occasionally be unavailable because of app updates, third-party outages, scheduled maintenance, or network problems outside Hrayem’s control.
