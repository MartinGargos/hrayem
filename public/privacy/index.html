Privacy Policy

This page explains what data Hrayem uses to run the app, why we use it, and what happens when a player updates or deletes their account.

What Hrayem does

Hrayem helps players create and join badminton, padel, and squash games, share event links, chat with confirmed players, post availability, and manage their own profile.

Data we process

  • Account data needed for sign-in, password recovery, and session management.
  • Profile data such as name, city, preferred language, and optional photo.
  • Sport profile data such as skill level, games played, no-shows, and thumbs-up stats.
  • Event data, event participation records, availability signals, and event chat messages.
  • Push notification preferences and push-token registrations for your devices.
  • Consent records for the current Terms of Service and Privacy Policy versions.
  • Reports submitted for events or players, plus delivery logs for admin review email.
  • Operational error data needed to keep the app reliable, including Sentry crash reports.

Why we use this data

  • To authenticate users and keep sessions working across app launches.
  • To show player identities, stats, and availability inside the app.
  • To run event joins, waitlists, chat, notifications, reminders, and reporting.
  • To detect abuse, investigate user reports, and keep audit records where required.
  • To maintain product quality, debug failures, and improve launch readiness.

Where data is stored and processed

Hrayem currently relies on Supabase for authentication, database storage, realtime updates, and file storage. Push notifications are delivered through Expo. Admin report emails are delivered through Resend. Crash reporting is handled through Sentry when that integration is enabled for a release build.

How long data stays in the system

Future-looking activity such as availability posts and push-token registrations is cleaned up when it expires or when account deletion succeeds. Historical participation, chat history, thumbs-up records, reports, and audit data may remain with deleted user references anonymized as “Deleted User” so past events and integrity checks stay understandable.

Your controls

Inside the app, you can update your profile, language, city, sport skill levels, notification preferences, availability, and account state. You can also delete your account from Settings. When account deletion completes, future organized events are cancelled, future joined events are cleaned up, availability and push registrations are removed, and the remaining historical records are anonymized where the product requires them for integrity.

Changes to this policy

Hrayem stores the accepted Terms and Privacy versions server-side. If the policy changes in a later release, the app can require renewed consent before a signed-in user continues.
